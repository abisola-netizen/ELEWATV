About ELEWA TV

Where African Creativity Meets the World Stage.

Elewa means "beautiful" — and for us, beauty is a creative act of resistance. Founded by women, powered by the diaspora, ELEWA TV has evolved from a creative network into a full-scale visual production company, with an inaugural film slate debuting in 2027.

We produce. We fund. We platform. Narrative film, documentary, branded content — always rooted in African and diasporic experience.
